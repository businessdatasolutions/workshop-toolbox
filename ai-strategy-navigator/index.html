Facilitator Notes

Round 1: Strategic Analysis and Formulation

  • Introduce the concept of strategy as the "logic of success for the organisation"

  • Reveal the first market dynamics card

  • Introduce a second market dynamics card
  • Groups focus on long-term sustainability of their strategy
  • Final strategy presentations (8-10 minutes per group)
  • Facilitate concluding discussion focusing on key learning objectives

AI Strategy Navigator Instructions

Game Objective

To develop and justify a successful long-term strategy for a hypothetical organisation by strategically integrating Artificial Intelligence (AI) to achieve a sustainable competitive advantage and secure necessary resources in a dynamic market.

Gameplay (3 Rounds)

Round 1: Strategic Analysis and Formulation

  1. Introduction & Understanding Strategy: Emphasize strategy as the "logic of success for the organisation"
  2. Scenario Immersion: Each group reviews their assigned Scenario Brief
  3. Exploring AI Capabilities: Groups examine the "AI Capability Cards"
  4. Developing the Initial Strategy Argument: Complete the "Strategy Argument Canvas"
  5. Brief Strategy Outlines: 2-3 minute overview of initial strategic direction

Round 2: Market Dynamics and Strategic Adaptation

  1. Introduction of Market Dynamics: Reveal a "Market Dynamics Card"
  2. Re-evaluating and Adapting Strategy: Groups discuss implications of the market event
  3. Refining the Strategy Argument: Revise the "Strategy Argument Canvas"
  4. Constructive Debate (Peer Review): Groups pair up and critique each other's strategies

Round 3: Long-Term Vision and Sustainability

  1. Second Market Dynamics Card: Introduction of another market shift
  2. Forecasting and Long-Term Sustainability: Consider longer-term implications
  3. Final Strategy Argument Presentation: 8-10 minute presentations
  4. Facilitated Discussion and Debrief: Focus on key learning objectives

Key Learning Objectives

  • What is Strategy? Strategy as a coherent logic of success, an argument built on assumptions.
  • Strategy vs. Other Business Games: Emphasizing long-term thinking, adaptation, and justification of choices.
  • AI from a Business Strategy Perspective: AI as a tool to achieve broader business goals, not as an end in itself.
  • The importance of identifying assumptions, adapting to market changes, and engaging in constructive debate.
